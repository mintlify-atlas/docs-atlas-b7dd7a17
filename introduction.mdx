## How jo works

Unlike cloud-based AI assistants that require you to upload files and explain your context, jo already knows your world:

- **Indexes locally**: jo connects directly to your Mac apps using the same APIs Apple uses for Spotlight
- **Processes on-device**: All AI processing happens on your Mac's Neural Engine—no cloud required
- **Understands context**: jo remembers what matters across your notes, emails, photos, and files
- **Answers instantly**: Because everything is local, results come back in seconds

<Note>
  jo uses Apple's M-series Neural Engine to run AI models directly on your hardware. This makes local AI fast, efficient, and completely private.
</Note>
