## About jo

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is jo and how does it work?">
    jo is a local AI assistant for Mac that runs entirely on your computer. It indexes your files, emails, and browsing history to provide context-aware answers without sending data to the cloud. Think of it as an AI that already knows your world.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is jo different from ChatGPT or Siri?">
    Unlike cloud-based assistants, jo runs locally and already knows your context—your files, emails, and browsing. No need to copy-paste or re-explain your situation each time. It's like having an AI with a perfect memory of your digital life.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does jo require an M-series Mac?">
    Apple's M-series chips (M1, M2, M3, M4) have a Neural Engine that makes local AI fast and efficient. jo uses this dedicated AI hardware to run models directly on your Mac without needing cloud processing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What data does jo access?">
    jo can index your local files, emails (via IMAP), and browser history. You control exactly what jo sees through granular permissions, and everything stays on your Mac.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data sent to the cloud?">
    Never, unless you explicitly choose to. jo runs entirely on your Mac. When you optionally call in a cloud model like GPT or Claude, only that specific conversation is sent—never your indexed data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does jo protect my privacy?">
    jo runs 100% locally on your Mac. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to send a specific conversation to a cloud model. No account required, no data collection, no tracking.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is jo free to use?">
    The jo app is free to download and use. Local AI queries are always free. You only pay for optional cloud model access (like GPT-5.2 or Claude) on a pay-per-use basis, typically $0.01-0.05 per conversation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does jo cost per month?">
    Most users spend less than $5/month on cloud model queries. Local queries are always free. There are no subscriptions—you only pay for what you use when you choose to call in more powerful cloud models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much do cloud models cost?">
    Pay-per-use, typically $0.01-0.05 per conversation. No subscriptions. Most users spend less than $5/month, and local queries are always free.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
