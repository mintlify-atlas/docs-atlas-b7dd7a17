jo is an AI that searches everything on your Mac—notes, photos, emails, messages, files—and finds what you need in seconds. Instead of matching keywords, jo understands meaning.

## How jo's search works

When you ask jo a question, it already knows your context. jo indexes your Notes, Photos, Mail, Messages, Calendar, and files locally on your Mac, so it's ready to help the moment you install it.

## What makes jo different

### vs. Spotlight

Spotlight matches exact words. jo understands meaning. Ask for "that email about money problems" and jo finds your budget discussions—even if you never used those words.

**Example:** You search for "that photo with the blue door from Portugal" from 2019. Spotlight fails because you never tagged it. jo finds it in 3 seconds because it understands what you mean.

### vs. ChatGPT

ChatGPT requires uploading files to external servers. jo already knows your stuff and never sends anything anywhere.

### vs. Rewind

Rewind records your screen (privacy nightmare). jo reads your actual files using the same APIs Apple uses. No screenshots, no recordings.

### vs. Note apps

Notion AI only searches Notion. jo searches Notes, Photos, Mail, Messages, and Calendar at once.

## Understanding vs. matching

Traditional search matches keywords. jo understands meaning.

<Info>
  **How it works:** jo uses AI to understand the semantic meaning of your question and your content. It can find things even when you don't remember the exact words.
</Info>

**Traditional search:**
- Searches for exact keyword matches
- Requires you to remember specific words
- Can't connect information across apps
- Returns a list of files

**jo's AI search:**
- Understands what you mean, not just what you say
- Finds relevant content even with vague descriptions
- Connects information across Notes, Mail, Photos, Messages
- Gives you answers, not just file paths

## Speed and privacy

jo indexes everything locally on your Mac. Nothing leaves your machine. Ever.

- **Fast:** Search results appear in seconds because everything is already indexed
- **Private:** Your data never gets uploaded anywhere
- **On-device:** The AI model runs locally on your Mac's Neural Engine
- **Always ready:** jo continuously indexes new content in the background

<Warning>
  jo runs 100% on your computer. Your notes, photos, and emails never get uploaded anywhere. Nothing is shared. Nothing leaves your machine. Ever.
</Warning>
